LGBTQIA+ inclusive health advocacy services

As an LGBTQIA+ individual, you deserve respectful, personalized care. From finding providers to understanding benefits, we’re here to guide you with compassion and confidentiality.

Not sure where to start? That’s okay.

· Get answers to your health questions, no matter how big or small

· Explore care options that fit your life, your goals, and your needs

· Find the right support and resources for your health and well-being

We offer expert help for LGBTQIA+ inclusive health and benefits navigation. We can help you:

· Find LGBTQIA+ affirming doctors and therapists who understand your values and needs

· Coordinate care across doctors, specialists, and insurance providers

· Guide you on preventive care like screenings, vaccinations, and wellness visits

· Understand your insurance, from what’s covered to handling billing issues or denied claims

· Get support for your sexual health, including STI testing, prevention, treatment, and understanding your options

· Walk you through options for gender-affirming care like hormone therapy or surgery

· Connect you with Centers of Excellence for advanced or specialized care

· Access HIV-related services including testing, prevention (like PrEP), and treatment

· Find LGBTQIA+ friendly support, including community programs and eldercare resources

We’ll listen, answer your questions, and help you figure out the next steps for your care. Call us or visit us online to get started.

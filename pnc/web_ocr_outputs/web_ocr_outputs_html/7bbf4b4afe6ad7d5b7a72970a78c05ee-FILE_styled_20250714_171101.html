Make Your Fitness Program Membership Work for You

The Fitness Program gives you flexible options to help you live a healthy lifestyle.

Since you are a Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Oklahoma (BCBSOK) member, the Fitness Program is available exclusively to you and your covered dependents (age 16 and older) .* The program gives you access to a nationwide network of fitness locations. Choose one location close to home and one near work, or visit locations while traveling.

Are You Ready for Fitness?

It’s easy to sign up:

1. Go to bcbsok.com and log in to Blue Access for MembersSM.

2. Select the Wellness tab on the top navigation bar of the Dashboard page. Then scroll down to the Fitness Program section and click on Learn More.

3. Complete registration form.

4. Verify your personal information and method of payment. Print or download your Fitness Program membership ID card. You may also request to receive the ID card in the mail.

5. Visit a fitness location today!

Prefer to sign up by phone or have questions about the Fitness Program? Just call the toll-free number 888-762-BLUE (2583) Monday through Friday, between 7 a.m. and 7 p.m., CT (6 a.m. and 6 p.m., MT).
